About Gamers Pipeline

Gamers Pipeline specifically targets low-income students and under-resourced schools where the vast majority of learners qualify for federal meal assistance. We establish partnerships with Title I school districts serving economically disadvantaged communities and veterans in transitional programs—populations traditionally excluded from affordable game development education. Through geographic focus on low-income neighborhoods and rural areas designated as economically distressed, we ensure that between seventy-five and eighty percent of the individuals and households we serve are classified as low-to-moderate income. This concentration is documented through school lunch eligibility records, veteran benefit status, and residential location in federally-recognized low-income census tracts.

The Problem

Gamers Pipeline tackles the lack of inclusive, career-focused STEAM and CTE programs by giving Title I schools and underprivileged youth the tools and mentorship they need to access high-paying tech and creative jobs. For veterans, it bridges isolation and career transition challenges with hands-on game development, technical skill-building, and a supportive peer community. Overall, the company closes industry access gaps by linking real-world opportunity to groups left out of traditional tech pathways.

Our Solution

Gamers Pipeline gives Title I schools and underprivileged youth a ready-to-use Unreal Engine system that any educator can teach, enabling students to build professional-quality multi-genre game portfolios meeting STEAM and career-readiness standards. For veterans, it combines structured game development training with community support and social events that harness gaming's healing power while preparing them for job placement in tech careers.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive game development education solutions for K-12 students, Title 1 youth, and Veterans.

STEAM & CTE Curriculum

Project-based STEAM and CTE curriculum designed for K-12 students, Title 1 youth, and Veterans. Develop technical skills and career readiness in game design and machine learning through engaging, real-world learning experiences.

Digital Assets

Exclusive digital assets for Unreal Engine and Unity that support cross-genre learning, enabling students to build skills progressively across multiple game development disciplines.

Community Building

Connecting veterans and first responders through competitive gaming, building community bonds, and fostering lasting camaraderie with peers who share their experiences.

Student Progression Path

Our curriculum is structured to support a clear progression for students at different grade levels.

6-8

  • Industry-ready portfolios
